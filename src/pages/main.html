About

I began programming through the Dongjak Innovation Camp, and later worked at a company participating in the Tourism Enterprise Innovation Voucher Program, where I contributed to digital transformation initiatives and public procurement projects (KONEPS/Nara Marketplace).

At a medical imaging company, I redesigned core logic and optimized rendering performance. By establishing observability through structured logging points across diverse scenarios and validating consistency based on those logs, I achieved a 92% reduction in positional error, a 79% improvement in p95 latency, and a 90%+ reduction in Long Tasks.
